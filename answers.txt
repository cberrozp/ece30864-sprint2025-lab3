1. What are some Pros and Cons of agile development (vs non-Agile)? List 2 each.
  Pros of Agile Development:
    More resilient to scope changes in the project.
    Allows for constant collaboration with Customers.
  Cons of Agile Developemtent:
    Less predictable.
    Requires high levels of collaboration.
  Pros of Non-Agile Development:
    Good for fixed requirements.
    More predictable than Agile Development.
  Cons of Non-Agile Development:
    It is Costly.
    It is Risky.

2. Explain the role and responsibilities of the Scrum Master vs the Product Owner
  The role of the Scrum Master is to facilitate the process by which people use Scrum. His responibilities are to remove roadblocks, keep the team productive and facilitate Scrum Events.
  The role of the Product Owner is to prioritize work. His responsibilities include to work on the Project backlog, accept new tasks and assignments and make sure that the work is done efficiently.

3. What are the types of meetings that happen during a sprint cycle? List at least 2 points on what needs to be discussed during each of these meetings.
  a. Sprint Planning Meeting
    Determine what to complete in the coming sprint
    Move stories in sprint scope from backlog to current sprint
  b. Daily Scrum/Standup Meeting
    Short meeting that happens every day of the sprint
    Team members discuss work completed, work plan for the day and any blockers
  c. Sprint Retrospective Meeting
    Occurs at the end of the sprint
    Discuss work completed and move incomplete work to backlog

4. Differentiate between Epic, Story, Bug, and Task issue types in JIRA
  An Epic captures a large body of work that can be broken into several smaller stories.
  A Story is a software system requirement that can be expressed in a few short sentences.
  A Task represents a set of work that needs to be done.
  A Bug is a problem that impedes one of the issues from being completed.
